Hello.

I am a recent mechanical engineering graduate from the University of Alberta and currently based out of Edmonton. Due to my passion for innovative technology, I would love to work for a design studio that allows me to apply mechanical design principles to consumer, industrial, and commercial products that are changing the world.

My most recent work stint was at John Deere where I designed, prototyped, and manufactured production tools used in transmission assembly lines. Oftentimes, the process involved bouncing ideas back and forth with technicians and turning an idea or sketch drawn on a napkin into something tangible the next day. My past experiences have instilled the importance of leveraging user input and utilizing user-centered design, lessons I carry with me to this day.

I can gladly say that I am a product of my environment. Growing up in a landlocked and cold Canadian province like Alberta has made me appreciate any opportunity I get to enjoy the outdoors. Shredding gnar pow on my snowboard, running trails, or a mouth full of sand after playing beach volleyball is when I am the happiest.
